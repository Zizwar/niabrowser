Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Important Notice

NIABrowser is designed with privacy in mind. This application operates primarily on your device and does not collect personal data for external purposes.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Local Data Storage

NIABrowser stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Browsing history and bookmarks
  • Custom JavaScript scripts you create
  • App settings and preferences
  • Network request logs for debugging purposes
  • Website cookies and local storage (standard browser behavior)

1.2 No External Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information to external servers. All data remains on your device unless you explicitly choose to share it.

2. How We Use Information

The locally stored information is used to:

  • Provide core browser functionality
  • Maintain your browsing history and preferences
  • Execute your custom scripts and debugging tools
  • Improve your user experience within the app

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose your data to third parties. Your browsing data, scripts, and settings remain private to you.

3.2 Website Interactions

When you browse websites through NIABrowser:

  • You interact directly with those websites
  • Websites may collect data according to their own privacy policies
  • NIABrowser acts as a transparent proxy for these interactions

4. AI Script Generation

If you use the AI script generation feature:

  • Your prompts are sent to OpenRouter.ai for processing
  • You must provide your own API key
  • We do not store or access your API communications
  • Please review OpenRouter.ai's privacy policy for their data handling practices

5. Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • API keys are stored securely using device encryption
  • No external transmission of sensitive data
  • Regular security updates and improvements

6. Data Control

6.1 Data Management

You have full control over your data:

  • Clear browsing history, cookies, and scripts at any time
  • Export or backup your custom scripts
  • Uninstall the app to remove all data

6.2 Data Portability

Your scripts and settings can be exported and imported, giving you full ownership of your configurations.

7. Third-Party Services

NIABrowser may interact with external services:

  • Websites: Direct interaction based on your browsing
  • OpenRouter.ai: Optional AI script generation (requires your API key)
  • GitHub: For source code and updates (no personal data transmitted)

8. Developer Responsibilities

As a developer tool, you are responsible for:

  • Scripts you create and execute
  • Websites you interact with
  • Compliance with website terms of service
  • Ethical use of debugging and automation features

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on our website and GitHub repository.

10. Open Source Transparency

NIABrowser is open source software. You can review our source code on GitHub to verify our privacy practices and data handling.
