Information We Collect

webZview is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary for the app to function:

  • Browsing history and bookmarks (stored locally on your device)
  • User scripts and settings (stored locally on your device)
  • Network logs and developer tools data (stored locally, not transmitted)

How We Use Your Information

All data collected by webZview is used solely for:

  • Providing browser functionality and navigation
  • Enabling developer tools and debugging features
  • Storing your preferences and settings
  • Managing user scripts and extensions

Data Storage and Security

Your privacy is important to us:

  • All data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms
  • No personal data is transmitted to external servers
  • Network monitoring data is used only for debugging purposes
  • You can clear all stored data at any time through the app settings

Third-Party Services

webZview may interact with third-party websites and services:

  • Websites you visit may have their own privacy policies
  • User scripts may access external resources
  • We recommend reviewing privacy policies of websites you visit

Data Retention

Data retention in webZview:

  • Browsing history and bookmarks are retained until manually cleared
  • Network logs are automatically limited to the most recent 100 entries
  • User scripts and settings persist until manually removed
  • You have full control over data deletion through the app interface

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data stored by the application
  • Delete any or all stored data at any time
  • Control which scripts and features are enabled
  • Use safe mode to disable all data collection features

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on our website. Continued use of webZview after policy changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
